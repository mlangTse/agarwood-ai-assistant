> An incense burner ( 香炉, kōro ) used for an ayamekō 菖蒲香 game, a part of kōdō 这是一种用于“菖蒲香”游戏的香炉，属于“香道”的一部分。 ***Kōdō*** (香道, "Way of Fragrance") is the art of appreciating Japanese incense, and involves using incense within a structure of codified conduct. *Kōdō* includes all aspects of the incense process, from the tools (香道具, *kōdōgu*), to activities such as the incense-comparing games *kumikō* (組香) and *genjikō* (源氏香).[^1] *Kōdō* is counted as one of the three classical Japanese arts of refinement, along with *ikebana* for flower arrangement, and *chadō* for tea and the tea ceremony.[^2] 香道，即“香之艺术”，是一种欣赏日本香料的方式。它要求人们在特定的礼仪规范下进行香料的运用。香道涵盖了香料的制作全过程，从所需的工具到各种与香相关的活动，比如比较香味的游戏“组香”和“源氏香”等。 [^1] 香道
