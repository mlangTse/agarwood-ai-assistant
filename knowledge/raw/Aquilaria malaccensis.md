***Aquilaria malaccensis*** ([Hindi](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hindi_language "Hindi language"): अगर, *agaru*; [Assamese](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Assamese_language "Assamese language"): সাঁচিগছ, *sānci*; [Arabic](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Arabic_language "Arabic language"): عود هندي, transl. Indian oud) or **agar** is a species of [lign-aloe tree](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Aquilaria "Aquilaria") in the family *[Thymelaeaceae](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Thymelaeaceae "Thymelaeaceae")*. It is found in [Bangladesh](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bangladesh "Bangladesh"), [Bhutan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bhutan "Bhutan"), [India](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/India "India"), [Indonesia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Indonesia "Indonesia"), [Laos](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Laos "Laos"), [Malaysia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Malaysia "Malaysia"), [Myanmar](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Myanmar "Myanmar"), the [Philippines](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Philippines "Philippines"), [Singapore](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Singapore "Singapore"), also [Thailand](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Thailand "Thailand"). It is threatened by [habitat loss](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Habitat_loss "Habitat loss").[^4]

*The World List of Threatened Trees* (Oldfield et al., 1998) listed [Iran](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Iran "Iran") as one of the countries with a population of *A. malaccensis*. The exploratory 2002 [CITES](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/CITES "CITES") review confirmed that Iran has no record of the species. As a result, Iran is no longer considered as habitat for or producer of [agarwood](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Agarwood "Agarwood").[^5]

## Uses

### Agarwood

*Aquilaria malaccensis* is the major source [^6] of [agarwood](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Agarwood "Agarwood"), a resinous heartwood, used for perfume and incense.[^2] The resin is produced by the tree in response to infection by a parasitic [ascomycetous](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ascomycetous "Ascomycetous") mould, *[Phaeoacremonium](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Phaeoacremonium "Phaeoacremonium") parasitica*,[^7] a [dematiaceous](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Black_yeast "Black yeast") (dark-walled) fungus. Agarwood is widely used in perfume industry, especially amongst Arabic brands. However, some European brands also claim to use natural agarwood oil in almost every perfume (for example: Montale [^8] and its perfume Wild Aoud [^9]).

### Sanchipat

The bark of *Aquilaria malaccensis* is used in the traditional [Assamese](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Assamese_culture "Assamese culture") manuscript, the *sanchipat.* It is a strung manuscript similar in construction to a [palm-leaf manuscript](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Palm-leaf_manuscript "Palm-leaf manuscript"). The bark of *Aquilaria malaccensis* is degummed and treated with toxic minerals to make it anti-fungal and pest resistant, helping it last in [Assam's](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Assam "Assam") humid tropical environment.[^10]  
阿奎拉里亚·马拉肯西斯树的树皮被用于阿萨姆文手稿的编写中。这种手稿属于卷轴式手稿，其构造与棕榈叶手稿类似。阿奎拉里亚·马拉肯西斯树的树皮经过去胶处理，并添加了一些有毒矿物质，从而使其具有抗真菌和抗虫害的能力，使其能够适应阿萨姆邦潮湿的热带环境。 [^10]

## Threats 威胁

Due to rising demand for agarwood, as well as shortcomings in monitoring harvests and an increasing illegal trade, *A. malaccensis* is on the brink of extinction in the wild and is now considered [critically endangered](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Critically_endangered "Critically endangered") on the IUCN Red List. Due to large-scale logging operations, many forested areas where *A. malaccensis* was once abundant have been destroyed.[^11]  
由于琼脂木的需求不断增加，加上对采伐量的监管不力以及非法贸易的加剧，A. malaccensis 在野外已经濒临灭绝，目前在国际自然保护联盟的红名单中被列为极危物种。由于大规模的伐木活动，许多曾经拥有大量 A. malaccensis 的森林区域已经被破坏掉了。 [^11]

### Conservation 保护

Despite its endangerment, *Aquilaria malaccensis* is highly adaptable and can also perform well in areas contaminated by pollution. Due to this, conservation plans have been set in place to raise agarwood in contaminated areas as well as homestead gardens.[^11] India is promoting the sustainable cultivation of *Aquilaria malaccensis* to support the economic growth of the North eastern states. [\[1\]](https://www.botanyknowledge.com/2026/02/indias-agarwood-boom-farmers-and.html)  
尽管 Aquilaria malaccensis 面临濒危的威胁，但它具有很强的适应性，能够在受污染的地区良好生长。因此，人们制定了保护计划，在受污染的地区以及家庭花园中种植琼脂木。印度正在推广 Aquilaria malaccensis 的可持续栽培方式，以推动东北部各州的经济增长。\[1\]
