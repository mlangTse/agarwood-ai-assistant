***Aquilaria crassna*** is a species of [plant](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Plant "Plant") in the [Thymelaeaceae](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Thymelaeaceae "Thymelaeaceae") family. It is [critically endangered](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Critically_endangered "Critically endangered") and native to [Southeast Asia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Southeast_Asia "Southeast Asia").[^1]

## Economics

*Aquilaria crassna* is one source [^2] of agarwood, a resinous heartwood, used for perfume and incense.[^3] The resin is produced by the tree in response to infection by a parasitic [ascomycetous](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ascomycetous "Ascomycetous") mold, *Phaeoacremonium parasitica*,[^4] a [dematiaceous (dark-walled) fungus](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Black_yeast "Black yeast").
