***Gyrinops*** is a genus of nine species of [trees](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tree "Tree"), called **lign aloes** or **lign-aloes trees**, in the family [Thymelaeaceae](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Thymelaeaceae "Thymelaeaceae").[^2] They are native to [Southeast Asia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Southeast_Asia "Southeast Asia"), the [Indian subcontinent](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Indian_subcontinent "Indian subcontinent"), and [New Guinea](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/New_Guinea "New Guinea").[^1]

The genus *Gyrinops* is closely related to *[Aquilaria](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Aquilaria "Aquilaria")* and in the past all species were considered to belong to *Aquilaria.*[^3]

## Agarwood production

Together with *Aquilaria* the genus is best known as the principal producer of the [resin](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Resin "Resin") -suffused [agarwood](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Agarwood "Agarwood").[^4] [^5] The depletion of wild trees from indiscriminate cutting for agarwood has resulted in the trees being listed and protected as an [endangered species](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Endangered_species "Endangered species").[^6] [^5] [^4]

Projects are currently underway in some countries in southeast Asia to infect cultivated trees artificially to produce agarwood in a sustainable manner.[^6] In Indonesia, for example, there have been proposals to encourage the planting of *gahara*, as it is known as locally, in eastern Indonesia, particularly in the [province of Papua](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Papua_\(province\) "Papua (province)").[^7]

## Species 物种

Nine species are accepted.[^1]

- *[Gyrinops caudata](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Gyrinops_caudata&action=edit&redlink=1 "Gyrinops caudata (page does not exist)")* (Gilg) Domke
- *[Gyrinops decipiens](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Gyrinops_decipiens&action=edit&redlink=1 "Gyrinops decipiens (page does not exist)")* Ding Hou
- *[Gyrinops ledermanii](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Gyrinops_ledermanii&action=edit&redlink=1 "Gyrinops ledermanii (page does not exist)")* Domke
- *[Gyrinops moluccana](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Gyrinops_moluccana&action=edit&redlink=1 "Gyrinops moluccana (page does not exist)")* (Miq.) Baill.
- *[Gyrinops podocarpa](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Gyrinops_podocarpa&action=edit&redlink=1 "Gyrinops podocarpa (page does not exist)")* (Gilg) Domke
- *[Gyrinops salicifolia](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Gyrinops_salicifolia&action=edit&redlink=1 "Gyrinops salicifolia (page does not exist)")* Ridl.
- *[Gyrinops versteegii](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Gyrinops_versteegii&action=edit&redlink=1 "Gyrinops versteegii (page does not exist)")* (Gilg) Domke
